# Meeting Summary Worker Bundle

This bundle collects the work-agent materials for the meeting summary workflow in one place.

## Structure

- `README.md` — original exported bundle overview
- `guides/` — operator docs, quickstarts, prompts, and runbooks
- `training/` — canonical prompt/spec/training corpus and sample outputs

## Why this lives in a bundle

These files are tightly related and are easier to browse together than as flattened top-level guides.
