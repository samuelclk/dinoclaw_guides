Lido Earn Product Overview: Samuel C explained that Lido earn consists of two sets of DeFi vaults, utilizing DeFi strategies for ETH and USD stablecoins. A 'curator,' which acts like a DeFi fund manager, such as Melo, decides how to allocate capital for these strategies. For ETH strategies, a looping strategy is used where staking ETH yields the liquid staking token stETH, which is then used as high-quality collateral to borrow more tokens, allowing for repeated staking loops (00:02:27).
Lido Earn Strategy Risk and Returns: The high loan-to-value (LTV) ratio for the stETH collateral, potentially up to 90%, enables multiple looping iterations. However, the product is institutionally focused, and Melo, as the curator, maintains a conservative risk approach, resulting in an estimated annual percentage rate (APR) of between five to seven percent, or a maximum of eight percent (00:03:52). Samuel C clarified that for all legal intents and purposes, Lido does not have a say in the specific assets Melo curates, as this would make them equivalent to a fund manager (00:04:56).

Invenium Capital's Structure and Mandate-Driven Approach: Jackson Blau detailed that Invenium Capital closed on the acquisition of MASA Partners, an ADGM-based asset management firm, bringing in their former Chief Investment Officer (CIO), who has managed over 700 billion in institutional assets. Focus on Real World Assets (RWAs) and Asset Types: Invenium Capital's investment opportunities are purely focused on Real World Assets (RWAs), including private credit, real estate, treasuries, private equity, and trade finance—anything off-chain that they plan to bring on-chain. These assets are sourced primarily from the UAE region, but with global sourcing as well, including the US (00:09:56). The strategy involves a 'barbell' approach to liquidity, where high-yielding but liquid assets, such as high-yielding exchange-traded funds (ETFs), are tokenized via their platform Swarm to serve as a liquid component in structured products alongside other private market assets (00:10:44).
