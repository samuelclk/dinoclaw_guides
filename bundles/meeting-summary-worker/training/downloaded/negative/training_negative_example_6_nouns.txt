Summary
Samuel C, who is traveling to Japan for Japan Fintech week to assess the Web3 market for potential collaboration, and Inter Noun discussed Lido's strategic directions, including the customization-focused SD vaults and the general user product Lido Earn, whose core focus remains liquid staking. Inter Noun confirmed the closing of a deal with the Nudge project to integrate Lido Earn into their quest platform, with product work beginning around March 1st, while Samuel C noted that Lido could not provide meaningful inorganic incentives to Nudge due to LDO token distribution, but could offer increased fee-sharing tiers based on Total Value Locked. Inter Noun also shared that the Nouns DAO has committed to deploying 1,000 Ethereum into an Octant permissionless vault and is considering an SD vault strategy with Lido for an additional 3,000 Ethereum in Q2, with Samuel C positively evaluating the Concrete vault infrastructure due to its sustainable strategies.

Details
Travel Plans and Context for Japan: Samuel C is traveling to Japan for Japan Fintech week and has been traveling since the start of the month, indicating a focus on international engagement. The purpose of the trip is to lay groundwork in the Web3 space, as major Japanese asset managers are reportedly advocating for regulators to permit them to hold a small percentage of digital assets. Samuel C intends to assess the direction of the Japanese market to understand how they can collaborate with local players, especially concerning Lido’s plans to offer stable coin earning strategies as part of Lido Earn.
Lido’s Core Focus and New Directions: The core focus for Lido remains liquid staking (Lido core), with two new strategic directions being SD vaults and Lido Earn. SD vaults are designed for institutions requiring a higher degree of customization, while Lido Earn is intended for general users, although institutions are expected to adopt it eventually. For institutional engagement, integration with custodians, node operators, and wallet front interfaces is identified as the necessary path forward, as institutions typically require outsourcing of liability and risk via service agreements.
Protocol Stability and Token Holdings: Lido prefers that users utilize Lido core over SD vaults because the Total Value Locked (TVL) in SD vaults should not exceed Lido core’s TVL, as this could destabilize the protocol. Samuel C indicated they do not possess public information regarding the percentage of Lido DAO (LDO) tokens held by Lido team members or contributors, confirming that their personal holdings of staked ETH (stETH) are a fraction of what institutions or whales typically deposit.
Discussion on the Nudge Project: Inter Noun confirmed that a deal with the Nudge project has been closed, with the term sheet signed, and product work scheduled to begin around March 1st after the Chinese New Year. The goal is to integrate Lido Earn into the Nudge product, which functions as a quest platform for users to reallocate funds to earn rewards or APY, with rewards provided by the projects themselves, such as Lido. Nudge acts as a reallocation platform and will not hold user tokens or money, aiming to incentivize users by offering Nudge points on top of the direct project incentives.
Challenges with Lido Incentives: Samuel C noted a challenge regarding providing inorganic incentives from Lido, explaining that over 90% of the LDO tokens have already been distributed, leaving a very small available float, much of which is already earmarked for contributors. As a result, the APR seen on Lido Earn is primarily organic, and Lido cannot offer meaningful additional incentives to the Nudge protocol. However, Samuel C proposed they could pursue increasing fee-sharing tiers for Nudge if the protocol is able to bring in certain amounts of TVL.
Nudge Monetization Strategy and Treasury Allocation: The Nudge team does not want to take any incentives directly from the projects, as this would be against their ethos; instead, they plan to pass on any shared fees directly to the users. The main monetization model for Nudge will be the Token Generation Event (TGE), which is scheduled for September. Nudge plans to allocate 20% of its total tokens to the Nouns treasury, which will be used to incentivize the Nouns community for providing TVL.
Nouns DAO and Vault Strategy: The Nouns DAO is considering building a liquid staking vault, but this is tentatively planned for the second half of the year, possibly October, after a planned $50 million fundraising for Nouns. They are likely to work with a third party, such as professional node operators, due to limited internal capacity, as the entire DAO is currently run by only two or three individuals.
Evaluation of Concrete Vault Infrastructure: Inter Noun inquired about Concrete, a project building vault infrastructure, where Nouns is deploying some capital. Samuel C expressed a positive view of Concrete, praising their organic and sustainable strategies, which involve funding rate and supply rate arbitrage across different on-chain protocols. This preference is based on a contrast with leverage looping strategies, which, while effective under normal market conditions, can become unviable during sudden volatility spikes.
Nouns’ Initial Capital Deployment Plan: The Nouns DAO has made a hard commitment to deploy 1,000 Ethereum into an Octant permissionless vault. Octant operates as an aggregator, deploying capital across different strategies, and a portion of the 1,000 Ethereum will be deployed to Concrete through the Octant vault. Inter Noun clarified that the Nouns DAO has another 3,000 Ethereum to allocate in Q2, where they would be open to collaborating on an SD vault strategy with Lido.
Inter Noun’s Personal Shift in Focus: Inter Noun mentioned a personal shift in focus for the immediate future, which includes exploring new opportunities and potentially joining another team as a contributor to learn from large Ethereum projects. Their previous focus was on onboarding institutions to Nouns, which is currently self-sustaining. They noted that the Nouns DAO, where they are the largest voting block, aims to grow the treasury slowly, with the expectation of becoming a top 10 treasury in the world this year due to pre-signed deals.
Next Steps for Lido and Nudge Integration: For the Nudge integration, Samuel C committed to speaking with the Lido Earn team to negotiate and push for a fee-sharing tier and then setting up a direct group chat between the Nudge product team and the Lido Earn team around early March. The Lido team will seek to understand Nudge’s background, their go-to-market strategy, and their plan for increasing TVL distribution for the new Lido Earn meta vault.
SD Vaults and Strategic Ethereum Reserve: Samuel C suggested that Nouns DAO could mint stETH and deposit it into their own SD vaults, allowing them to negotiate preferential fee arrangements with custodians, and then use that stETH to deposit into Concrete, or even compose the Concrete vault strategy directly onto the SD vaults. They suggested Inter Noun speak with Northstake, a node operator working with Lido, who has created a wrapper around SD vaults for easy setup, as Nouns has significant leverage to negotiate favorable terms due to their ability to bring in up to $100 million in community TVL.
