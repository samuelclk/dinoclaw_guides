Details
Introductions and Background of Toricico and P-Tech Labs: Samuel C. and Sinobu introduced themselves, with Sinobu explaining that they co-founded P-Tech Labs, which has operated as a DeFi development company since 2021. P-Tech Labs advises Toricico, a company for which a colleague, Motoi, is officially working as COO, regarding DeFi strategy and investment operations, including Ethereum purchases. Motoi joined Toricico as COO to push the Ethereum DeFi investment strategy internally, and Sinobu joined the call due to their knowledge of Lido.
P-Tech Labs' Pivot and Projects: P-Tech Labs recently pivoted to a curator model, and they are currently hosting the JPY market on Mofo. The company has developed several DeFi projects, including the smart contract insurance protocol InsurAce and Orange Finance, which provides liquidity for AMM, options, and derivatives. Although none of their projects achieved major success, the co-founders shifted their focus to the traditional finance sector, supporting a dating company.
Lido Staked ETH (stETH) Eligibility: Samuel C. suggested that Toricico add Lido stETH into the eligible markets for their vault, as supplying collateral on Mofo does not currently earn interest, making raw ETH less desirable as collateral compared to stETH. Sinobu confirmed that they are already suggesting this inclusion because raw ETH collateral does not earn any fees or interest in Mofo.
Samuel C.'s Role and Lido Overview: Samuel C. leads the Asia-Pacific region for the Institutional Relations team at Lido, working with Ethereum DAOs, ETF/ETP issuers, custodians, exchanges, and asset managers to explore capital-efficient ways to use ETH and build products on top of stETH or Lido. Samuel C.'s background includes venture capital, the CFA charter, and being an Ethereum enthusiast and contributor since 2017.
Motoi's Background and Toricico's Business: Motoi Takahashi shared their background in venture capital, focusing on technology like crypto and DeFi, and they co-founded the DeFi development company with Sinobu in 2021. Motoi is now in charge of managing the Ethereum treasury for Toricico, a publicly listed Japanese company, whose core business is e-commerce for manga and hosting events for anime. Toricico has about 7,000 Ethereum and leverages influential shareholders and distribution channels to educate people on Ethereum and DeFi.
Toricico's Fundraising and Strategy: Toricico is raising funds from hedge funds and asset managers using moving strike warrants, with a focus on asset managers as the main target. They are partnering with EVO funds, which supported Meta Planets from the beginning. Toricico is highly interested in becoming a node operator on top of Lido to support stETH, and they plan to set up a US entity, accumulate ETH, and aim to earn staking rewards without fees, using their influence to encourage Japanese users to delegate to their vault.
Custodial Requirements and US Entity Setup: Samuel C. inquired about Toricico's current qualified custodians, to which Motoi replied that they use SBI VC Trade and Coincheck, which has a custodian license in Japan. For the US entity, Toricico plans to contract with different custodians, such as Anchorage, Copper, or BitGo, because Japanese regulations require 95% of funds to be in a cold wallet. The custodians must support stETH rebasing because the quantity of stETH changes daily, and Samuel C. offered to help facilitate the integration with custodians by positioning Toricico as a large Japanese DAO.
Lido Node Operator Proposal and Parameters: Becoming a Lido node operator is permissionless, but receiving better parameters requires submitting a proposal detailing the organization, intended use of stETH vaults, and plan for bringing in Total Value Locked (TVL). The proposal is evaluated by a committee that determines the reserve ratio, where the lower the ratio, the more stETH can be minted from the vault's TVL for use in DeFi to enhance yields. The maximum minting ratio is around 98%, with the most lenient reserve ratios (2-4%) designated for a DVT cluster.
Lido V2 vs. V3 and Target Users: Samuel C. explained that users can still use Lido V2 for a 1:1 stETH minting ratio, but V3, while offering a less preferential ratio, allows institutions to designate a qualified custodian and their own node operators, which is often necessary for regulatory compliance. For retail users and DeFi users, V2 is generally better, but V3 allows for the creation of tailored, curated DeFi strategies on top of the stETH vault that minimize user friction.
DVT Cluster and Fundraising Strategy: The reserve ratio requirement is based on the amount of stETH to be minted; the more stETH minted, the higher the required reserve ratio for protocol safety. The first step for Toricico's next finance tech is to get identified, be assigned a category and reserve ratio by the VOs committee, and then develop a detailed DeFi strategy. By integrating stETH vaults with Mofo, Toricico can increase revenue sources, making the investment strategy more appealing to potential investors during fundraising.
Co-Marketing and Business Development (BD) Efforts: Lido is bound by legal constraints regarding co-marketing due to its permissionless nature, meaning external projects cannot expect a high level of co-marketing, such as press releases, until trust and confidence are built over time following an integration. However, Samuel C. has flexibility with on-the-ground BD efforts and offered to join fundraising conversations to present Lido to investors, which can add credibility to Toricico's pitch.
StETH Looping Strategies and Layer 2 Challenges: Toricico is developing looping strategies using stETH, noting that they are considering Optimism because its borrowing rate is lower than on Ethereum mainnet. The challenge with Optimism is the bridging cost and the exit time of up to seven days to return to Ethereum, compounded by low liquidity of stETH/ETH on platforms like Uniswap and the risk in emergency cases. Samuel C. offered to check with the protocol relations team for workarounds related to the leverage looping strategy on Optimism.
Next Steps and Meeting Schedule: Samuel C. agreed to send links for the proposal submission process to help Toricico get identified and confirmed that they can reach out for support during the filling process. Samuel C. also mentioned being in Japan next week and they arranged to meet on Tuesday, the 24th, at 2:30 PM for coffee.
